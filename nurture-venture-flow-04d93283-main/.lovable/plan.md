

# Founders KSA — Website Plan

## Brand & Design
- **Color palette** derived from your logo: pink-to-orange gradient as the primary accent, with clean whites and soft grays for backgrounds
- **Your logo** embedded in the header/navbar
- **Modern, clean aesthetic** — sleek typography, generous whitespace, subtle gradient accents, smooth animations. Think polished startup/tech website feel.

## Pages & Sections

### 1. Home (Landing Page)
- **Hero section** with bold headline like "Your Gateway to Entrepreneurship in Saudi Arabia" with a gradient accent and a call-to-action
- **Services overview** — cards highlighting key offerings: Licensing Guides, Startup Ecosystem, Founder Stories
- **Featured Founder Stories** — a curated grid of 2-3 highlighted founder profiles
- **Quick stats/highlights** — e.g., industries covered, resources available
- **Footer** with navigation links, social media, and contact info

### 2. Licensing & Business Setup Guide
- Clean, organized sections covering:
  - How to get a **Personal License**
  - How to get an **Entrepreneurial License**
  - How to **open a company** in KSA
  - **Startup ecosystem** overview — how things work
- Easy-to-read layout with clear headings and step-by-step information

### 3. Founder Stories
- A grid/list page showing all founder story cards (with image, name, industry tag like Gaming, Healthcare, Biotech, etc.)
- Click into each story to read the full profile/interview
- Filter by industry category

### 4. About / Contact
- About section explaining the Founders KSA mission
- Contact form or contact details for inquiries

## Admin Area (For You Only)
- A simple **login page** (just for you as the admin — no public sign-up)
- Once logged in, you can:
  - **Create a new founder story** — add title, content, image, industry category
  - **Edit or delete existing stories**
  - **Manage licensing guide content**
- This requires a **backend** (Lovable Cloud with database) to store the stories and content

## Backend (Lovable Cloud)
- **Database** to store founder stories (title, content, images, industry, date)
- **Authentication** for admin-only login
- **Storage** for uploaded images

## What We'll Build First
We'll start with the full frontend — all 4 pages with beautiful design, your logo, and gradient colors. Then we'll add the backend (database + admin) so you can manage content yourself.
